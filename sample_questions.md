# Sample Questions for INDmoney RAG Assistant

Try asking these questions to see how the assistant handles different types of queries!

### 📈 Factual Data Queries
- "What is the current NAV of ICICI Prudential Large Cap Fund?"
- "What is the expense ratio of HDFC Small Cap Fund?"
- "Tell me the AUM of Kotak Large Cap Fund."
- "When was Mahindra Manulife Mid Cap Fund started?"
- "What is the portfolio turnover of Motilal Oswal Large and Midcap Fund?"

### ℹ️ Fund Information
- "Tell me about Bank of India Flexi Cap Fund."
- "What is the risk category of HDFC Flexi Cap Fund?"
- "Who are the fund managers for ICICI Large Cap?"
- "What is the return since inception for Mahindra Mid Cap?"

### 🛠️ Investment & Process
- "What is the minimum SIP and Lumpsum for HDFC Small Cap?"
- "Is there any exit load for Kotak Large Cap Fund?"
- "How can I invest in Bank of India Flexi Cap?"

### 🛡️ Guardrails & Limitations (What it WON'T answer)
- "What is the NAV of Parag Parikh Flexi Cap Fund?" (Bot will refuse - unsupported)
- "Which fund is best for me?" (Bot will refuse - no investment advice)
- "Should I buy ICICI Large Cap today?" (Bot will refuse - no timing advice)

- "What is the return on the HDFC Flexi Cap Fund?"
